Hey Tim, in our session on Tuesday your calves were really grumpy. I think we've talked about it before, but I know that one well. As recently as 2022 I couldn't do standing practice for more than ~3 min because of it.

The biggest lever for me was learning to coordinate the intrinsic muscles of the foot (the small muscles inside the foot itself) and to differentiate them from the extrinsic muscles (the bigger ones running down from the lower leg). TLDR When the foot does its own job, the calves stop overworking to hold you up. The exploration below is in a Feldenkrais-style, which is what I found most helpful in untangling the intrinsic/extrinsic relationship.
